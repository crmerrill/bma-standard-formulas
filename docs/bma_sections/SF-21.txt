5. Use of the SDA for Products Other Than 30-Year Conventional Mortgages
The SDA was designed for use with fully amortizing residential mortgages with a term of at
least 15 years. It was not intended to be used with other securitized products, e.g., balloon
mortgages, commercial mortgages, home equity loans or any nonmortgage assets such as
auto loans and credit card receivables.
